1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Data Pro Marketing LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), a Florida limited liability company, collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the FlipLogic AI service available at fliplogic.ai (the "Service").

FlipLogic AI is a deal analysis platform for fix-and-flip real estate investors. We take your privacy seriously, and we have designed the Service to collect only the data we need to operate it. This policy explains exactly what that means.

By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

Service Status

FlipLogic AI is currently in active beta. Our data practices may evolve as the Service matures. We will notify registered users of any material change to this policy by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account and use the Service, you provide us with the following information:

  • Email address and password (password is hashed and salted; we never see or store your plaintext password).
  • Property addresses and property details you enter into deals (bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, year built, etc.).
  • Financial inputs you enter for deal analysis (purchase prices, rehab estimates, ARV estimates, loan terms, closing costs).
  • Free-form notes you save in the Field Notes section of any deal.
  • Audio recordings, photos, and video you capture or upload through the AI Walkthrough feature.
  • Comparable sales (comps) data you enter or edit manually.
  • Optional support communications you send to support@fliplogic.ai.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Authentication tokens and session data.
  • Browser type, operating system, and device information needed for the Service to function.
  • IP address (used for security monitoring and rate limiting).
  • Timestamps of your interactions with the Service (account creation, deal creation, AI analyses, subscription events).

2.3 Payment Information

When you subscribe to a paid plan, payment information (credit card numbers, billing address) is collected and stored exclusively by our payment processor, Stripe, Inc. We never see, store, or transmit your full credit card number. We receive only a Stripe customer identifier and limited transaction metadata (subscription status, last four digits of card, billing email) needed to manage your subscription.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

To be clear about what we deliberately avoid collecting:

  • We do not collect your Social Security Number, date of birth, or government identification.
  • We do not collect bank account numbers or routing numbers.
  • We do not collect your full credit card information (Stripe handles this).
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers, pixels, or behavioral analytics services.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following limited purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To process AI-powered analysis of property walkthroughs (audio, photos, video) and generate scope-of-work findings.
  • To pull comparable sales and rental data for properties you analyze.
  • To process subscription payments and manage your billing.
  • To send transactional communications (account verification, password reset, billing receipts, service notifications).
  • To respond to your support requests and feedback.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders).

We do not use your data to train AI models. Property walkthroughs and other content you submit are processed by our AI vendors solely to return analysis results to you, not to improve or train the underlying models. (See Section 5 for our vendors' specific commitments.)

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored in cloud infrastructure operated by Supabase, Inc. on Amazon Web Services. Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases located in the United States.

4.2 Encryption

  • Data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher between your browser and our servers.
  • Data at rest is encrypted in our database via Supabase's managed encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt algorithms — we cannot recover or view your password.

4.3 Access Control

Your data is protected by row-level security in our database. Each user can only read or modify their own deals, comps, scope items, and walkthrough findings. Database administrators at Data Pro Marketing LLC have backend access for operational purposes (debugging, support, billing reconciliation) but do not routinely view individual user data.

4.4 Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Active accounts: data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Account deletion: when you request deletion, your deals, comps, walkthrough findings, and scope items are permanently deleted within 30 days. We retain billing records for the period required by law (typically 7 years for tax purposes).
  • Inactive accounts: accounts inactive for 24 months may be subject to deletion after 30 days' notice via email.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Portability

You can access all your deal data, comps, walkthrough findings, and scope items at any time through the Service. To request a structured export of your data in a machine-readable format, email support@fliplogic.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

6.2 Correction

You can correct any information you've entered into the Service directly within the app. For corrections to billing or account information, contact support@fliplogic.ai.

6.3 Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and associated data at any time. You may request deletion by emailing support@fliplogic.ai. (An in-app account deletion feature is on our roadmap and will be available before broad public launch.) Upon receipt of a verified deletion request:

  • All deals, comps, scope items, walkthrough findings, and notes are permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Any active subscription is canceled.
  • Your authentication record is removed.
  • Billing records required by tax law are retained for the legally required period.

6.4 California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect about you.
  • The right to delete your personal information (subject to legal exceptions).
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising — there is nothing to opt out of.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@fliplogic.ai.

6.5 European Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • The right of access to your personal data.
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate data.
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • The right to restrict processing.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to object to processing.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Our legal basis for processing your data is the performance of our contract with you (the Terms of Service). To exercise your GDPR rights, contact support@fliplogic.ai. We are not required to designate an EU representative because the volume and nature of our processing of EU residents' data is limited and incidental.

7. Cookies and Tracking

FlipLogic AI uses only the cookies and local storage strictly necessary for the Service to function:

  • Authentication cookies that keep you signed in across page reloads.
  • Local storage entries that remember your selected deal and your onboarding state.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, behavioral tracking pixels, social media widgets that track you, or analytics tools that profile users across sites. Because we use only essential cookies, we do not display a cookie consent banner — there is no non-essential tracking to consent to.

8. Children's Privacy

FlipLogic AI is intended exclusively for use by adults age 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact support@fliplogic.ai and we will investigate and delete the information promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers and most of our service providers are located in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, processed in, and stored in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent legal mechanisms for transfers from the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery, in accordance with applicable law. Notifications will describe the nature of the breach, the data potentially affected, the steps we have taken in response, and the steps you can take to protect yourself.

Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
